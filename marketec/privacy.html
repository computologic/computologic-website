At Computologic, we prioritize the privacy of our business users. This policy outlines how we handle data within the Marketec software ecosystem.

1. Marketec for PC (Windows Store Version)

Marketec for PC is a business tool for offline and cloud-synced data management. Data handling is as follows:

  • Personal Information: We collect Company Name (required), and optionally User Name and Email Address. This is used solely for license registration, trial authentication, and technical support.
  • Location Data: Marketec for PC does not access, collect, or transmit your device's geographical location.
  • Business Data: Your invoices, stock, and sales data are stored locally. Full license users may utilize our secure servers for cloud synchronization.
  • Support Backups: Database backups are only handled if you manually and voluntarily send them to us for support purposes.

2. Marketec Lookup (Android App)

The Marketec Android app serves as a mobile companion for business data. Its current data practices are:

  • Location Data: This application does not access, collect, or transmit any geographical location data from your mobile device.
  • User Identifiers: We collect Account IDs and Email addresses for account management and license verification.
  • Device Identifiers: We may collect device IDs for security purposes and to prevent unauthorized license usage.

3. Data Security and Retention

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using secure protocols (HTTPS/TLS).
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or share your personal or business data with third-party advertisers or external services.
  • Retention: We retain registration information for the duration of your active license. You may request data deletion at any time by contacting us.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access the data we hold about your account or request its deletion. For any privacy-related inquiries or data removal requests, please contact our support team.
