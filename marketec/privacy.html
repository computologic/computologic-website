At Computologic, we prioritize the privacy of our business users. This policy outlines how we handle data within the Marketec software ecosystem.

1. Marketec for PC

Marketec for PC does not collect any personal information automatically. Users request a license manually by emailing us. No data is transmitted from your device to our servers unless you voluntarily send it for support purposes.

2. Marketec Lookup (Android App)

The Marketec Android app is a mobile companion for businesses. Data handling is as follows:

  • User Identifiers: Only Client Key, User ID, and Password are used to connect to the Firebase server. No email or personal information is collected.
  • Location Data: This app does not access or transmit your device's location.
  • Device Identifiers: Not collected.

3. Data Security

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS).
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or share your personal or business data with third parties.
