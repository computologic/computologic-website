  • Business Data: Your invoices, stock, and sales data are stored locally on your device. Full license users may choose to store information online for cloud synchronization and license verification.
  • Support Backups: You may voluntarily send database backups to our server for support purposes; we do not request or automatically collect backups.

Marketec Lookup (Android App)

The Marketec Android app collects the following information to provide its core features:

  • Location: We collect Approximate and Precise Location to enable salesman check-in verification and route tracking. Location is only collected while the app is in use (Foreground) and is optional. You can deny location access at any time; core features will still work without it. Permissions requested: ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION.
  • User IDs: Account IDs and email addresses are collected for account management and license authentication.
  • Device Identifiers: We may collect device IDs for app functionality, security, and preventing misuse.

Data Security: All data is encrypted in transit using secure protocols. Location and personal information are never shared with third-party advertisers or external services.

Data Retention: Location data is retained only as long as needed for check-in and route tracking and is automatically deleted afterward.
