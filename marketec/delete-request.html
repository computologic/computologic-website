Marketec Data Deletion Request

This page facilitates data deletion requests for the Marketec Lookup app, developed by Computologic.

Steps to Request Deletion

To permanently delete your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Enter your Client Key and Username in the form below.
  2. Click "Submit Request" to generate an email to our support team.
  3. Our team will verify the credentials and complete the deletion within 30 days.

Data Handling & Retention

What is deleted: Upon a successful request, Computologic will permanently delete:

  • User Authentication Credentials (Username and Password)
  • Location Data: All stored Precise and Approximate location history
  • User IDs: Your unique Firestore identifier
  • App Performance & Crash Logs

What is kept: We may retain anonymized transaction records or data required for legal, regulatory, or fraud prevention purposes as per our primary business license agreement.

Retention Period: Manual deletion requests are processed within 30 days. Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered.
